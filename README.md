# Cohort — iOS app

Premium LTR dating app (Cohort). This repo contains the **native SwiftUI iOS app**, product docs, and guidelines.

## Contents

- **`ios/`** — SwiftUI iOS app (Xcode project). See [ios/README.md](ios/README.md) for how to open, build, and run.
- **`Documents/`** — MVP PRD, build plan, and first-principles brainstorm.
- **`guidelines/`** — Design and AI guidelines.

## Running the iOS app

1. Open `ios/Cohort.xcodeproj` in Xcode.
2. Select the Cohort scheme and a simulator or your device.
3. Press **⌘R** to build and run.

To run on your own device, set your development team in **Signing & Capabilities** and use bundle ID `com.ayanmukherjee.cohort` (or your own unique bundle ID).
